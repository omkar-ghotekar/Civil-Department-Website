NEERI EDUCATIONAL VISIT2019-20


Event Name : Neeri Educational Visit 2019-20


Dated:  20th September and 21 th September 2019

Description:

Neeri(Environmental Engineering Research Institute) located at Nagpur was an educational tour organized by airways on 20 & 21 of September 2019. This tour was organized to increase the knowledge and to understand the scope in Environmental Studies & Research. About 70 students visited and gained knowledge about it. National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (CSIR-NEERI) is a research institute created and funded by the Government of India. It was established in Nagpur in 1958 with focus on water supply, sewage disposal, and communicable diseases and to some extent on industrial pollution and occupational diseases found common in post-independent India. This visit was really helpful and we all gained a lot of knowledge and inspiration to look further in this field.

VISIT TO GARGOTI MINERAL MUSEUM


Event Name : Visit to gargoti Mineral Museum


Dated:  1st February 2020

Description:

As geological studies are an integral part of any civil engineering project, the visit has invoked our interest in the subject and we got an opportunity to witness a rare collection of minerals and rocks housed in the museum.It was scheduled on 1st february 2020. ‘Gargoti’ The Mineral Museum is located at 32 kms from Nashik on Nashik-Shirdi highway in a small bustling town of Sinnar in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Sinnar is located at 19.85°N 74.0°E. It has an average elevation of 651.4 metres (2135 feet). Sinnar is one of the major industrial zones of Malegaon (MIDC) built around the city of Nashik which has multiple international production companies. The objective of visit was: To understand the natural process of formation of minerals and rocks. To understand the stratigraphy of Maharashtra- “The Deccan Traps” which is one of the largest continental flood basalt provinces on earth. To identify the main rock forming minerals of the earth crust.

VISIT TO SEWAGE TREATMENT PLANT


Name of Event -: Visit to SEWAGE TREATMENT PLANT


Dated:  29 Sep 2019

Description:

The students of BE Civil Engineering, Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering and Research Ravet, Pune visited sewage treatment plant on 20th Sep 2019 at 10:30 AM. The first sewage treatment plant of its kind producing biogas based Power and Hydro Power. The capacity of the treatment plant is 30 MLD which runs on Modern SBR technology with power generation at Akurdi. The visit was very informative. Mr. Rahul Sawate sir explained the working of sewage treatment plant. Students studied and observed the working of sewage treatment plant which is completely automated and operated with very less manpower. Conventional SBR is improved by introducing Combi-Treat Unit ahead of CLSBR (Constant Level SBR) Unit to generate power from Bio-gas and to reduce organic load in CLSBR basins. I-SBR technology has potential to generate power from two sources: Power from Biogas through Combi-Treat unit by providing Bio-Gas engines, and Power from Hydraulic head available for treated sewage while flowing to discharge.
