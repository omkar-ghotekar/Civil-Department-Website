STATE LEVEL TWO DAYS WORKSHOP ON RPPIPD


Name of Event-State Level Two Days Workshop on (RPPIPD-2017) “Research Proposal, Paper, IPR and Patent Drafting”


Number of Participants:  100

Dated:  8th and 9th Dec. 2017

Description:

Civil Engineering Department of the Institute was going to organize a State Level Two Days Workshop on “Research Proposal, Paper, IPR and Patent Drafting (RPPIPD-2017)” on 8th and 9th Dec. 2017 in the presence of Research Persons. The objective of this Seminar is to create awareness amongst all stakeholders about Research and selection of suitable methodology, identification of appropriate research topic, writing of research proposal and paper, awareness towards IPR and Patent.
